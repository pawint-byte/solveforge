Peter Wint is the founder of Wint Enterprises and the creator of CryptoOwnBank, FamilyRoots, and SolveForge (pawint-app.com).
With more than 25 years of enterprise IT and cybersecurity leadership—including senior roles at Lockheed Martin (current Lead System Engineer on mission-critical radar programs), IBM, Incyte Pharmaceuticals (where he managed a $25M global network & security budget), AT&T, Raytheon, and Boeing—Peter has designed, deployed, and secured complex global infrastructures for government and Fortune 500 clients. He holds active CISSP and CompTIA Linux+ certifications, along with CCNA, CCDA, and Blockchain Essentials from Cornell University.
Peter has been active in cryptocurrency for years. As his portfolio grew across multiple blockchains and exchanges, he became frustrated by the lack of a single tool that could provide a complete, unified view—tracking wallets, cost basis, yield opportunities, and payments—while maintaining full self-custody. Drawing on his deep enterprise security and architecture experience, he built CryptoOwnBank to solve that exact problem for himself. He is now making it available to everyone who wants the same financial independence and control over their assets.
He has applied the same hands-on, problem-solving mindset to his other platforms:

FamilyRoots — a private, invitation-only family and group networking platform with interactive trees, AI-powered genealogy assistance, and collaboration tools that protect what matters most.
SolveForge — a transparent crowdsourcing marketplace that connects real-world technical, business, and creative challenges with expert proposals, milestone-based payments, and secure (including crypto) transactions.

Peter’s work is guided by a simple philosophy: technology should empower individuals, protect privacy, and give people real control over both their financial futures and personal legacies. At Wint Enterprises, he continues to turn hard-won enterprise expertise into practical, user-first tools for the decentralized world.
